Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Playlist Progress Saver is built around a simple principle: your watch history belongs to you, and it stays on your own machine unless you choose otherwise.

What we store

  • The playlist ID, video ID, and timestamp of playlists you choose to track.
  • Optional tags, categories, and colors you assign to a playlist.
  • Your Dark Mode / Light Mode preference.

Where it's stored

All of the data above is saved locally in your browser's extension storage. Playlist Progress Saver does not run its own server and does not transmit your playlist data anywhere by default.

Future cloud features

Planned Premium features such as Cloud Sync and Automatic Backup will require explicit opt-in before any data leaves your browser. This policy will be updated before those features ship.

Third parties

Playlist Progress Saver does not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or analytics companies.

Your data, your control

Use the built-in Export feature at any time to download a full copy of your data as JSON, or uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@playlistprogresssaver.com.
